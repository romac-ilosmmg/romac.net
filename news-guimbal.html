Romac Job Fair in Guimbal, IloIlo

Romac Visayas Services Inc. successfully joined the Local Job Fair in Guimbal, Iloilo, bringing exciting career opportunities closer to job seekers in the province. The event was organized in partnership with the Public Employment Service Office (PESO) Guimbal and the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE), aiming to connect applicants with trusted employers.

During the job fair, Romac opened various vacancies for local deployment, giving applicants the chance to explore career paths in hospitality, technical trades, and other industries. Hundreds of job seekers visited the booth, submitted their applications, and received initial interviews from Romac’s recruitment team.

This initiative reflects Romac’s continuous commitment to providing quality employment opportunities and supporting the local government’s efforts to reduce unemployment in the region.

Romac thanks the LGU of Guimbal, PESO, and DOLE for the successful collaboration and looks forward to future partnerships in creating more job opportunities for Filipinos.
