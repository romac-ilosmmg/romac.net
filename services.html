Services

Romac Visayas Services Inc.

We specialize in connecting people and businesses across the Visayas building meaningful partnerships that create growth and opportunity. At Romac Visayas Services Inc. ,our mission is two fold: to help companies find dependable, skilled manpower they can rely on, and to guide individuals toward rewarding careers that match their skills and aspirations.

Whether you are a five-star hotel in need of professional staff, an office seeking clerical support, or an individual aiming to start a career in janitorial, hospitality, or administrative services, Romac is here to provide the right solutions. Serving Iloilo, Bacolod, Boracay, Leyte, Samar, and Cebu, we are proud to be a trusted partner in empowering businesses and shaping futures across the region
