About Us

Who We Are

ROMAC, a company with a 41-year history, was founded in September 1980.Initially awarded small contracts by the 13th Air Force Contracting Center of the U.S. Air Force at Clark Air Base, ROMAC focused on repairing equipment for various U.S. government agencies stationed at the base.

In December 1995, ROMAC VISAYAS was established, operating in Iloilo and Bacolod before expanding to Cebu and Boracay. Known for its dependable manpower services in the Visayas Region, the company excels in Janitorial Services and has catered to esteemed companies in production and sales utilities.

In the Visayas region, ROMAC is the top choice for businesses in need of manpower services, including prestigious five-star hotels in Boracay. Offering a range of services and positions such as Janitorial, Clerical, Merchandising, Front-liners, Bakers,Baristas, Bartenders, Housekeeping attendants, Drivers, Boat Men, Messengers, and more, ROMAC VISAYAS not only provides training and job opportunities but also aims to improve the lives of its employees, enabling them to support their families. With a personalized approach, ROMAC values both its clients and its dedicated employees.
